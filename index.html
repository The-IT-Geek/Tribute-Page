Short Timeline for Bill Gates:

  • 1955 - Bill Gates is Born
  • 1967 - Bill Gates attends Lakeside School
  • 1973 - Bill Gates enrolls at Harvard University
  • 1975 - Bill Gates and Paul Allen form partnership called Micro-soft
  • 1976 - Bill Gates writes "Open Letter to Hobbyists"
  • 1978 - Microsoft opens first international office in Japan
  • 1979 - Microsoft moves headquarters to Bellevue, Washington
  • 1980 - Microsoft partners with IBM to write the BASIC for the IBM PC
  • 1981 - Microsoft becomes corporated and Bill Gates becomes president
  • 1989 - Bill Gates founds Corbis
  • 1994 - Bill Gates marries Melinda French
  • 1998 - United States VS Microsoft Trial
  • 2005 - Live 8 concerts
  • 2008 - Bill Gates steps down from daily duties
  • 2009 - TED 2009 held in Long Beach, CA
